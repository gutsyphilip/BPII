Projects

One Laptop Per Child

As the name suggests, One Laptop Per Child is a project to provide sufficient computer accessories for secondary schools in Ugbowor Local Government Area, Benin City. We have 16 schools mapped out to be reached with this project. Our goal is to empower the students to start acquiring digital skills at an early stage. We hold weekly sessions in schools that have been reached.
